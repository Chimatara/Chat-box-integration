Our Story

A Family Recipe, A Professional Service

Mama's Table was born in a small Lagos kitchen in 2012, when founder Matara began cooking for neighbours' celebrations out of sheer love for feeding people well. Word spread quickly from one street party to a full wedding, and from that wedding to corporate boardrooms across the city.

Today, our team of 20 trained chefs and event coordinators serves hundreds of guests at occasions across Nigeria. We bring the same warmth, precision, and bold flavours that started it all whether we're plating for 30 or catering a gala for 3,000.

Every menu is custom-built for your event. We source ingredients locally, cook everything fresh on the day, and stay until the last plate is cleared. Your celebration is our priority from the first enquiry to the final bite.
